Example of plug-in By-Page

The plug-in By-Page enables you to display only a part of the data.
There is also a plug-in Navigation Bar.

In this example, there are [var.RecCnt] records displayed per page of [var.PageSize].


N# Product Price
 [blk.#]
 [blk.product;block=tr]  
[blk.price;frm='0.00']

Number of products: [var.RecCnt]
